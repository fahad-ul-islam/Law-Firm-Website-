Our Expertise

Criminal Law

We provide full representation across all trial phases, including pre-arrest and post-arrest bail matters, trial advocacy, and appeals in High Courts and the Supreme Court. Our expertise covers white-collar crimes, narcotics cases, and cybercrime defense.

Civil Law

Our team ensures precision in pleadings and evidence for property disputes, damages claims, declaration suits, and specific performance. We handle complex civil litigation with a focus on quick resolution and effective remedies.

Constitutional Law

We specialize in Public Law, including Article 199 petitions, enforcement of fundamental rights, judicial review of administrative actions, and public interest litigation to challenge ultra vires state actions.

Service Law

We represent civil servants and public sector employees in disciplinary matters, promotion disputes, and removal from service appeals before Service Tribunals and High Courts.

Family Law

Compassionate handling of sensitive matters including divorce, khula, maintenance, child custody, guardianship, succession certificates, and inheritance disputes.

Election Law

Expert advisory and litigation services for election petitions, disqualification references, delimitation disputes, and representation before Election Tribunals and the ECP.

Anti-Harassment Laws

We provide legal protection and representation in workplace harassment cases under the Protection against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act, defending victims and advising organizations on policy.

Banking Law

Representation in Banking Courts for recovery of loans, finance disputes, mortgage issues, and regulatory compliance under the Financial Institutions (Recovery of Finances) Ordinance.

Corporate & Commercial Law

From company incorporation to corporate governance, mergers, shareholder disputes, and contract drafting, we provide comprehensive legal support to businesses and startups.

Labour & Employment Law

Handling industrial disputes, workmen compensation, unfair dismissal, and statutory compliance matters before Labour Courts and the NIRC.

Taxation Law

Advisory and litigation services regarding income tax sales tax, assessments, audits, and constitutional challenges to fiscal statutes.

Intellectual Property Law

Protection of intellectual assets including copyright registration, trademark infringement, patent licensing, and obtaining injunctions against violations.

Consumer Protection Law

Filing and defending complaints regarding defective products, unfair trade practices, and substandard services before Consumer Courts.

Environmental & Energy Law

Guidance on environmental compliance, EPA hearings, energy sector disputes, and litigation regarding environmental hazards and regulations.

Arbitration & ADR

Strategic representation in domestic and international arbitration, mediation, and drafting of effective Alternative Dispute Resolution clauses.
